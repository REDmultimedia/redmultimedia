About The Project

While many things have gone digital there is still a need for traditional printing practices in business. Things such as Trade Show Booths, Business Cards, Letterhead, etc. Are just some of the examples printing needs that a company would have. Understanding printing best practices and how to get the job done right the first time ultimately saves time and money. Especially when it comes to large scale printing like Trade Show booth which can cost thousands of dollars to get made.

In my time working for J2 Global and Contiki Vacations. I was presented with several opportunities to create large scale print projects. Working closely with various departments and the vendor I was able to create unique one-of-a-kind graphics that would go on to help support sales and marketing initiatives across many different segments of company business.
