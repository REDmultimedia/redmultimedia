About The Project

I was contacted by Leslie Jee Textiles (LJT) to help them complete their existing website. Their original developer had gone radio silence on them during the process and left them high and dry. Fortunately I was able to step in and do a thorough assessment of their wants and needs to determined the best course of action. Ultimately we decided based on those needs that it would be easier to replace their WordPress theme with a better more robust theme.

All in all this was a fantastic project that provided me the opportunity to learn how setup and integrate WooCommerce. As well as how to do some jQuery and PHP customizations. The client was thrilled with the end result and I continue to maintain and help them to update the website.
