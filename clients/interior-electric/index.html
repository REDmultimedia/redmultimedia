About The Project

Interior Electric contacted me to help them update their existing website at the time. After having an initial conversation with them and doing a bit of research into their old web solution. I determined that the best course of action would be to migrate away from what they had and instead opt for a WordPress website that they could then maintain and update moving forward. I handled all aspects of this website from design to code.

The clients most important needs was to have a single consolidated place for people to get contact information for their offices in California, Nevada, and Arizona. As well as to provide a central place for potential customers to submit Energy Audit Forms and Requests for Estimates. All of which was done using forms that would then be submitted to the corresponding department for fulfillment. Which served to automate a lot of what they were doing by hand previously.

This was a great project that allowed me to really flex my creative as well as technical skillsets and deliver a beautiful one of a kind user experience that my client loved.
